About

With an eyes closed passion for a tender folk ballad,
Daniel Pell is an emerging artist brimming intensity.

Mindful of the masters, his tenor tone and acoustic
instrumentation stir the soul with the songs that
tell stories that must be told.

His influences range from Bill Monroe in the
blugrass world to classic rock groups like Led Zeppelin.

It seems that Daniel Pell was raised to play music,
reach out, and make people feel something.

His goal is to make you laugh, consider, and maybe even cry.

Young, raw, and expressive, his rhythmic performances touch the heart.
